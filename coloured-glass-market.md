<p><ul><li>Coloured Float Glass</li><li>Coloured Plain Plate Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coloured glass market comprises various segments, notably coloured float glass and coloured plain plate glass. Coloured float glass is created by adding metal oxides during the melting process, resulting in vibrant, transparent sheets used for architectural and decorative purposes. In contrast, coloured plain plate glass is typically thicker, non-reflective, and is often used for windows and furniture. Both types cater to diverse applications in construction, automotive, and interior design, driven by aesthetic appeal and functionality.</p></p>
