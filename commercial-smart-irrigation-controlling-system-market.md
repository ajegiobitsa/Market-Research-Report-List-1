<p><p>The Commercial Smart Irrigation Controlling System market is primarily segmented into two types: weather-based and time-based controlling systems. Weather-based systems utilize real-time weather data to optimize watering schedules, reducing water waste and ensuring plants receive adequate moisture. In contrast, time-based systems operate on a fixed schedule, regardless of environmental conditions. While time-based systems are simpler and easier to implement, weather-based systems offer more efficiency and adaptability, making them increasingly popular among commercial users focused on sustainable water management.</p></p>
