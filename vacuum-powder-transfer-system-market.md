<p><p>The Vacuum Powder Transfer System market is primarily categorized into two types: mechanical conveyors and pneumatic conveyors. Mechanical conveyors utilize physical mechanisms, such as belts or screws, to move powders, ideal for heavy or bulk materials. In contrast, pneumatic conveyors employ air pressure to transport powders through pipes, suitable for lighter materials and environments where contamination is a concern. Both systems enhance efficiency and safety in powder handling processes across various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food, and chemicals.</p></p>
