<p><p>The carbon steel pipe fitting market consists of two primary types: hot rolled and cold rolled. Hot rolled fittings are manufactured at high temperatures, which makes them easier to shape and more cost-effective, but they may have rougher surfaces and lower dimensional precision. In contrast, cold rolled fittings are produced at room temperature, resulting in better surface finish and tighter tolerances, but at a higher cost. Both types serve various applications in construction, oil and gas, and manufacturing industries.</p></p>
