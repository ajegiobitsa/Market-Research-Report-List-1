<p><p>The dental filling instruments market is divided into two main types: metal and plastic. In contrast, plastic instruments are lightweight and non-reactive, suitable for procedures involving delicate tissues or where aesthetics matter. Both types cater to different dental requirements, offering varied benefits to ensure effective and comfortable patient care in restorative dentistry.</p></p>
