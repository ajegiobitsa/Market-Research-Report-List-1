<p><strong>The Medical & Hygiene Non Woven Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drylaid</li><li>Airlaid</li><li>Wetlaid</li><li>Spunlaid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical and hygiene non-woven fabric market includes four main types: drylaid, airlaid, wetlaid, and spunlaid. </p><p>Drylaid involves laying fibers in a dry state, offering high strength and bulk. Airlaid uses air to create a web of fibers, ideal for absorbent products. Wetlaid combines fibers suspended in a liquid, resulting in a soft, absorbent material. Spunlaid processes molten polymer into continuous filaments, providing durability and lightweight options. Each type serves specific applications in medical and hygiene products, ranging from disposable items to surgical materials.</p></p>
