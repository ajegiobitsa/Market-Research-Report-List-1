<p><p>Employee onboarding software is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based onboarding software is hosted online, allowing easy access from different locations and enabling real-time updates, making it suitable for remote workforces. On-premises software, on the other hand, is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but often requiring more IT resources. Organizations choose between these types based on their specific operational needs and IT infrastructure capabilities.</p></p>
