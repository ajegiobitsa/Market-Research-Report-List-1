<p><p>The infrared imaging seeker market is divided into two main types: refrigeration and non-refrigerated. Refrigerated seekers utilize cooling systems to enhance sensitivity, enabling them to detect faint thermal signatures, making them ideal for high-performance applications like missiles and advanced surveillance systems. Non-refrigerated seekers, on the other hand, operate without cooling and are generally smaller, lightweight, and more cost-effective, suitable for applications where size and budget constraints are critical, such as in drones or automotive systems.</p></p>
