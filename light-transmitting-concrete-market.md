<p><p>The light-transmitting concrete market comprises two primary types: fiber light-transmitting concrete and resin light-transmitting concrete. Fiber light-transmitting concrete incorporates optical fibers, enabling light to pass through while maintaining structural integrity, making it ideal for creative architectural applications. In contrast, resin light-transmitting concrete uses a resin matrix to embed light-conducting materials, offering flexibility in design and enhancing visual aesthetics. Both types are increasingly popular in modern construction, illuminating spaces and adding unique design elements to buildings.</p></p>
