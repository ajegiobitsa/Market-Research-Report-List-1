<p><p>The OTC tests market primarily includes two key types: immunoassays and lateral flow assays. Immunoassays utilize antigen-antibody reactions to detect specific substances in a sample, offering high sensitivity and specificity, often used in diagnosing conditions like infections or hormonal imbalances. Lateral flow assays, known for their simplicity and rapid results, involve a sample applying to a test strip, where capillary action facilitates the detection of target analytes visually. Both test types enhance accessibility and convenience in healthcare diagnostics.</p></p>
