<p><strong>The Remote Afterloading System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HDR Afterloaders</li><li>PDR Afterloaders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The remote afterloading system market is primarily divided into two types: HDR afterloaders (High Dose Rate) and PDR afterloaders (Pulse Dose Rate). HDR afterloaders deliver high doses of radiation in a short period, allowing for precise treatment of tumors while minimizing exposure to surrounding tissues. In contrast, PDR afterloaders administer lower doses in a pulsed manner, providing a continuous therapeutic effect over a more extended period. These systems enhance treatment efficiency and patient safety in radiation therapy.</p></p>
