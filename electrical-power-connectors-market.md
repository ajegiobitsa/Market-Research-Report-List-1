<p><p>The electrical power connectors market is primarily divided into two categories: DC connectors and AC connectors. DC connectors are designed for direct current applications, commonly used in renewable energy systems, battery storage, and electric vehicles, ensuring stable power transfer. In contrast, AC connectors are suited for alternating current applications, widely used in residential, commercial, and industrial settings for powering various devices and machinery. Each type is engineered to meet specific voltage, current, and safety standards, facilitating efficient and reliable power distribution.</p></p>
