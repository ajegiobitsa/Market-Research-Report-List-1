<p><strong>The Medical Grade Acrylic Pressure Sensitive Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Emulsion Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical grade acrylic pressure sensitive adhesives market is categorized into solvent-based and emulsion-based types. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong adhesion and are ideal for high-performance applications, providing durability and resistance to moisture. However, they can pose environmental and health risks due to volatile organic compounds. Emulsion-based adhesives, on the other hand, utilize water as a solvent, making them more environmentally friendly. They provide good adhesion with lower toxicity, making them suitable for various medical applications such as wound care and device attachment.</p></p>
