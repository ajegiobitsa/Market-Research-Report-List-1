<p><p>The fixed panoramic sunroof market comprises two main types: laminated glass and tempered glass. Laminated glass features a composite structure that enhances safety by holding shattered pieces together, providing increased protection against impacts and noise reduction. Tempered glass, on the other hand, is heat-treated for superior strength and resilience, shattering into small, less harmful pieces upon breakage. Both types offer unique advantages, including improved aesthetic appeal and enhanced passenger experience, contributing to the growing demand in the automotive industry.</p></p>
