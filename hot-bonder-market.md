<p><ul><li>Single Zone</li><li>Dual Zone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hot bonder market types are categorized into single zone and dual zone systems. Single zone hot bonders operate in one temperature-controlled area, suitable for simpler repairs or smaller parts, ensuring uniform heat application. In contrast, dual zone hot bonders feature two independent heating zones, allowing for more complex applications involving larger components or varied materials. This flexibility enables simultaneous restoration of different areas at optimal temperatures, improving efficiency and compatibility in advanced composite repairs.</p></p>
