<p><p>Paddy husker markets are categorized mainly into semi-pneumatic and pneumatic types. Semi-pneumatic huskers use a combination of mechanical and pneumatic systems, offering moderate efficiency and are suitable for smaller operations. In contrast, pneumatic huskers rely solely on air pressure for the husking process, providing higher efficiency, consistent quality, and better scalability for larger volumes. Both types aim to maximize rice yield while minimizing grain damage, catering to varied scales of rice processing operations.</p></p>
