<p><p>Non-Contact Chemical Concentration Monitors are categorized into "Inline Inspection" and "Offline Inspection" types. Inline Inspection involves continuous monitoring of chemical concentrations during production processes, ensuring real-time quality control and immediate adjustments. This method enhances efficiency and reduces waste. In contrast, Offline Inspection refers to periodic evaluation of samples taken from production lines, allowing for detailed analysis and calibration. While it provides comprehensive insights, it may not offer the immediacy of inline methods, potentially affecting responsiveness.</p></p>
