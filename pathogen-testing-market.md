<p><ul><li>PCR</li><li>LAMP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pathogen testing market encompasses various technologies, with two prominent methods being PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) and LAMP (Loop-mediated Isothermal Amplification). PCR is a widely used technique that amplifies DNA to detect pathogens with high sensitivity and specificity, making it suitable for clinical diagnostics. In contrast, LAMP operates at a constant temperature, allowing for rapid and efficient amplification, ideal for point-of-care testing. Both methods cater to diverse applications, including medical, food safety, and environmental testing.</p></p>
