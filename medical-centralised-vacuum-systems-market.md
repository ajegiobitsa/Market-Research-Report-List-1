<p><strong>The Medical Centralised Vacuum Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cyclonic Central Vacuum Systems</li><li>Filtered Central Vacuum Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical centralized vacuum systems market features two primary types: cyclonic and filtered central vacuum systems. Cyclonic central vacuum systems utilize centrifugal force to separate particulates from the air, minimizing maintenance and enhancing efficiency for medical applications. In contrast, filtered central vacuum systems incorporate filtration mechanisms to trap contaminants, ensuring cleaner air and preventing the spread of pathogens. Both systems are integral to maintaining hygiene in healthcare facilities, providing reliable suction for applications such as surgical procedures and laboratory use.</p></p>
