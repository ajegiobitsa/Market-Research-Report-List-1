<p><p>Aerial devices encompass various types of lifts designed for elevated access. Telescoping boom lifts extend straight upward, providing reach for vertical applications, while articulated boom lifts have joints that allow for navigation around obstacles. Scissor lifts offer a stable platform with a crisscross design, ideal for indoor tasks requiring height. Truck-mounted lifts combine mobility with aerial access, mounted on vehicles for easy transportation to job sites. Together, these devices enhance efficiency and safety in construction and maintenance operations.</p></p>
