<p><p>The cancer diagnostics market can be categorized into platform-based and instrument-based segments. Platform-based diagnostics involve comprehensive systems and technologies that integrate various testing methods, such as genomic profiling and liquid biopsies, to determine cancer presence and characteristics. In contrast, instrument-based diagnostics focus on specific devices or instruments, like imaging machines and molecular analyzers, used for direct detection and analysis of cancer. Both segments play crucial roles in enhancing diagnostic accuracy and personalizing cancer treatment strategies.</p></p>
