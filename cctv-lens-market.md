<p><p>The CCTV lens market primarily features two types: fixed focus lenses and zoom lenses. Fixed focus lenses have a set focal length, providing a consistent field of view, making them ideal for static monitoring. They are typically more cost-effective and simpler to install. In contrast, zoom lenses offer variable focal lengths, allowing for adjustable fields of view. This versatility enables users to focus on specific areas or objects, making them suitable for dynamic environments where detailed surveillance is necessary.</p></p>
