<p><p>The aquaculture lighting market primarily includes halogen and LED lighting types. Halogen lighting, known for its high-intensity and warm color temperature, is often used in specific aquaculture setups requiring strong illumination. However, it has higher energy consumption and shorter lifespan compared to LEDs. Conversely, LED lighting is increasingly favored due to its energy efficiency, longevity, and ability to provide customizable light spectra, which can enhance fish growth and health. Consequently, LED lighting is becoming the preferred choice in modern aquaculture.</p></p>
