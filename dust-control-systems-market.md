<p><p>The dust control systems market encompasses various types tailored to different operational needs. Mobile controllers are versatile units that can be relocated easily. Handheld controllers offer portability for smaller applications. Self-propelled systems are autonomous, moving independently to manage dust in larger areas. Tractor-mounted systems attach to tractors for efficient coverage in agricultural and industrial settings. Trailed systems are towed behind vehicles for dust suppression over extensive terrains. Fixed controllers are stationary installations designed for permanent dust management in specific locations.</p></p>
