<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Direct Diesel Injection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pump-Line-Nozzle Injection System</li><li>Unit Injector Injection System</li><li>Common Rail Injection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Direct Diesel Injection System market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Pump-Line-Nozzle Injection System**: This traditional system uses a pump to pressurize fuel, delivering it through lines to individual injectors, facilitating precise fuel delivery.</p><p>2. **Unit Injector Injection System**: Each injector combines the fuel pump and nozzle in a single unit, enhancing efficiency and reducing space requirements.</p><p>3. **Common Rail Injection System**: A shared high-pressure rail supplies fuel to multiple injectors, allowing for better control of injection timing and improved engine performance and emissions.</p></p>
