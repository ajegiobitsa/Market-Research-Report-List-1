<p><p>Broadband Power Line Communication (PLC) chipsets are categorized into two main types: standalone and hybrid. Standalone chipsets operate independently, directly connecting power lines to communication networks without additional support. They are typically simpler and cost-effective for basic applications. In contrast, hybrid chipsets combine PLC technology with other communication methods, such as Wi-Fi or Ethernet, enhancing flexibility and performance. This integration allows for improved data transmission and broader connectivity options, making hybrid solutions suitable for complex and high-demand environments.</p></p>
