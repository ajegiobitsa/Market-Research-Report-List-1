<p><p>The rigid endoscopic lithotripsy devices market includes several types focused on breaking down kidney stones. Ultrasonic lithotripsy uses high-frequency sound waves to fragment stones, offering precise targeting with minimal tissue damage. Ballistic lithotripsy employs mechanical energy to disintegrate stones through shock waves generated by a projectile. Other methods may include photo-disruption or laser-based techniques, each with unique mechanisms and applications. These technologies provide various options for effective stone management, catering to different patient needs and clinical scenarios.</p></p>
