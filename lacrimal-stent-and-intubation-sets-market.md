<p><p>The lacrimal stent and intubation sets market is categorized primarily into monocanalicular and bicanalicular stents. Monocanalicular stents are designed to be inserted into a single lacrimal duct, facilitating tear drainage in cases of obstruction or dysfunction. In contrast, bicanalicular stents are placed in both ducts, providing enhanced tear flow and stability. Both types are crucial in ophthalmic procedures to restore proper drainage and improve patient comfort, driven by growing awareness of eye-related health issues and advancements in medical technology.</p></p>
