<p><p>The furniture store software market is divided into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, offering easier access, scalability, and lower initial investment. This model allows for automatic updates and reduced IT responsibilities, making it popular among furniture retailers seeking flexibility and cost-efficiency.</p></p>
