<p><strong>The Biophotonic Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrinsic Sensor</li><li>Extrinsic Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biophotonic sensor market is categorized into intrinsic and extrinsic sensors. Intrinsic sensors utilize the biological properties of the sample being measured, offering high sensitivity and specificity for detecting biological signals. In contrast, extrinsic sensors employ external light sources and optics to analyze samples, allowing for more versatile applications and adaptation to various environments. Both types play critical roles in areas such as medical diagnostics, environmental monitoring, and food safety, each offering unique advantages for specific applications.</p></p>
