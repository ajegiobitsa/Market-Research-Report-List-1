<p><p>The personalized knee replacement market comprises three primary components: femoral, tibial, and patellar implants. Femoral implants are designed to replace the thigh bone's end, ensuring optimal fit and function. Tibial implants serve as the base for the knee joint, providing stability and support. Patellar implants replace the kneecap, enhancing movement and alignment. These personalized solutions leverage advanced imaging and manufacturing technologies to create customized implants that improve patient outcomes and satisfaction by addressing individual anatomical variations and functional needs.</p></p>
