<p><p>Silicon steel sheets are categorized into two main types based on their magnetic properties: oriented and non-oriented. Oriented silicon steel sheets have a specific grain direction, enhancing their magnetic performance, making them ideal for high-efficiency applications like transformers and electric motors. In contrast, non-oriented silicon steel sheets exhibit isotropic magnetic properties, providing consistent performance in various directions, and are commonly used in general applications like power tools and appliances. Both types serve essential roles in the electrical industry.</p></p>
