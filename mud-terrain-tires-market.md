<p><p>Mud terrain tires are specifically designed for off-road vehicles, providing enhanced traction in muddy and uneven conditions. The market consists mainly of two types: radial tires and bias-ply tires. Radial tires feature ply layers that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering better flexibility, fuel efficiency, and durability. In contrast, bias-ply tires have layers that crisscross, delivering superior load capacity and puncture resistance, making them ideal for extreme off-road conditions. Both types cater to distinct user needs and preferences.</p></p>
