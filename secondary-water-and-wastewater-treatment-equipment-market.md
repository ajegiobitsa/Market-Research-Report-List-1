<p><p>The secondary water and wastewater treatment equipment market primarily includes activated sludge systems and sludge treatment technologies. Sludge treatment focuses on managing the byproducts from primary and secondary treatment, including thickening, digestion, and dewatering, to reduce volume and prepare sludge for disposal or beneficial reuse. These technologies are essential for enhancing water quality and promoting environmental sustainability.</p></p>
