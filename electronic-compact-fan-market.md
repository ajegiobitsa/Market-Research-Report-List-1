<p><strong>The Electronic Compact Fan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Fan</li><li>Axial Fan</li><li>Mixed Flow Fan</li><li>Cross Flow Fan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic compact fan market encompasses various types, each serving distinct applications. Centrifugal fans utilize a rotating impeller to generate airflow, ideal for high-pressure settings. Axial fans move air parallel to the fan's axis, offering efficient cooling in compact spaces. Mixed flow fans combine characteristics of both centrifugal and axial designs, providing versatility in airflow and pressure. Cross flow fans feature a unique design that enables airflow across their width, making them suitable for applications requiring uniform ventilation in confined areas.</p></p>
