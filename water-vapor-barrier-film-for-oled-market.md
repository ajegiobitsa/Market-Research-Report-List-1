<p><ul><li>Organic Barrier Film</li><li>Inorganic Barrier Film</li><li>Composite Barrier Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED market features three primary types of water vapor barrier films: organic, inorganic, and composite. Organic barrier films utilize carbon-based materials, offering flexibility and easier fabrication. Inorganic barrier films, made from materials like silicon oxide, provide superior moisture resistance and durability but can be less flexible. Composite barrier films combine both organic and inorganic materials, leveraging the strengths of each to achieve optimal moisture protection while maintaining flexibility and lightweight characteristics, making them suitable for diverse OLED applications.</p></p>
