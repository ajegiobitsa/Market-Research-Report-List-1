<p><strong>The Gas Detector Calibration Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bump Testing</li><li>Full Calibration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas detector calibration service market mainly comprises two types: bump testing and full calibration. Bump testing involves exposing the gas detector to a known concentration of gas to verify its response and functionality. This process ensures that the detector is responsive and can provide accurate readings in real-world scenarios. Full calibration, on the other hand, is a more comprehensive process that adjusts the detector's sensitivity and accuracy according to manufacturer specifications, ensuring optimal performance and reliability in gas detection.</p></p>
