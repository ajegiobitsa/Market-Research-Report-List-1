<p><p>The toggle and fastening clamp market can be categorized into horizontal and vertical types based on their design and application. Horizontal clamps operate with a lever mechanism that moves parallel to the work surface, offering stability and ease of use, often preferred in assembly and woodworking. In contrast, vertical clamps function by clamping perpendicular to the surface, ideal for applications requiring compact designs and quick access. Both types serve essential roles in industrial and manufacturing settings, enhancing efficiency and precision.</p></p>
